Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Chef Mario Rossi Italian Cuisine Expert

A creamy and delicious pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Ready in just 20 minutes.

20 min
Ingredients
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh black pepper
  • Salt for pasta water

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated cheeses, and black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
  5. Add hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta and remove from heat.
  6. Quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water gradually to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.
